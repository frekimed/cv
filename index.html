Cody Staub, RN, CEN

Special Programs Coordinator at Kittitas Valley Healthcare

Cody is an emergency nurse and Special Programs Coordinator at Kittitas Valley Healthcare in Ellensburg, Washington. His role includes coordinating the sepsis, trauma, stroke, and cardiac programs at KVH in addition to working as a charge nurse in the ED. He is active at the State and Regional level in various advisory councils and committees. He is also very active in the Emergency Nurses Association, serving on both State and National committees. In his free time, Cody loves to make, break, and build things and can often be found in his shop or at the local maker space.
